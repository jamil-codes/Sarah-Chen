ABOUT
About

Design is how
I think.

I'm Sarah — a UI/UX designer and brand strategist based in Toronto. I've spent 6+ years at the intersection of aesthetics and function, helping startups and scale-ups build products that earn trust on first sight.

My process starts with understanding real users, runs through rigorous iteration in Figma and Framer, and ends only when every pixel earns its place. I believe great design is invisible — it just feels right.

When I'm not designing, I'm teaching a workshop at the Toronto Design Club, hiking the Bruce Trail, or reading dense UX literature so you don't have to.
